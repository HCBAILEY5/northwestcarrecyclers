Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying parts and selling your vehicle.

Buying Parts

Do all parts come with a warranty?+
Yes — all parts supplied by Northwest Car Recyclers come with a 3-month warranty as standard. If a part develops a fault within this period, we'll replace or refund. Peace of mind as standard.
How do I know the part will fit my vehicle?+
Our team will ask for your full vehicle details — make, model, year, engine size and ideally the VIN — to ensure we match the correct part. We'd rather take extra time upfront than supply an incorrect part.
Are your parts tested before sale?+
Yes. All parts are checked and tested as part of our dismantling process. Electrical items such as ECUs, alternators and starter motors are tested before dispatch. We never supply parts we wouldn't use ourselves.
Can you deliver parts to me?+
Yes — we offer UK-wide delivery on most parts. Smaller items are posted via tracked courier. Larger items such as engines and gearboxes can be delivered by specialist freight. Delivery charges vary by item and destination.
What if you don't have the part I need?+
If we don't currently have your part in stock, we'll do our best to source it. We have good relationships with other quality parts suppliers and dismantlers across the UK, giving us access to a wide network of stock.
Do you supply to trade customers?+
Absolutely. We welcome trade accounts from garages, workshops and motor factors. Trade customers benefit from competitive pricing, priority stock access and reliable service. Contact us to discuss a trade account.

Selling Your Vehicle

What types of vehicle do you buy?+
We purchase cars, vans and light commercial vehicles in any condition — running or non-running, accident damaged, MOT failures, end-of-life and unwanted vehicles. All makes and models are considered.
How much will you pay for my vehicle?+
Prices depend on the vehicle's make, model, age, mileage and condition, as well as the current demand for its parts. We aim to offer fair, competitive valuations. Complete our online form or call us for a fast quote.
Do you collect vehicles?+
Yes — we offer free vehicle collection across Lancashire and the wider Northwest. In some cases we may be able to collect further afield. Collection is arranged at a time convenient for you.
What documents will I need?+
You will need the vehicle's V5C logbook (registration document), proof of your ID and confirmation that you are the registered keeper. We'll guide you through the process when you contact us.
How quickly will I be paid?+
Payment is made quickly and securely on collection of the vehicle. We don't delay payment — once the vehicle and paperwork are handed over, you receive your money.
Can I sell a vehicle that isn't running?+
Yes — non-running vehicles are welcome. We buy vehicles that won't start, have seized engines, blown head gaskets or other serious mechanical issues. Contact us with the details and we'll provide a fair valuation.
